# Texas Used Vehicle ROI Analysis
### South Central Texas Automotive Group | San Antonio, TX | 2026

---

## Business Problem
South Central Texas Automotive Group acquires, repairs, and resells used vehicles 
in the San Antonio market. Every acquisition decision — which car to buy, at what 
price, and whether the repair cost is worth it — is currently based on experience 
and gut feel rather than data.

This project replaces that guesswork with a data-driven acquisition model. By 
analyzing the Texas used vehicle market, we identify which makes, models, and price 
ranges deliver the highest ROI and fastest turnover, giving the business a repeatable 
framework for smarter buying decisions.

---

## Key Business Questions
1. Which makes and models sell fastest in the San Antonio market?
2. Which vehicles hold value best relative to estimated repair cost?
3. What is the average days-on-lot by vehicle category and price range?
4. Which price ranges move fastest and generate the highest margins?
5. Are there seasonal patterns in what sells and when?
6. Which vehicle types have the highest spread between acquisition cost and resale price?

**Target output example:**
> *"Ford F-150s in the 2018-2021 range priced between $18,000-$24,000 sell in an 
> average of 12 days with an estimated ROI of 22%, making them the highest-priority 
> acquisition target in the San Antonio market."*

## Data Sources
- **CarGurus** — San Antonio used vehicle listings (price, mileage, days on market, condition)
- **Craigslist San Antonio** — Private seller listings and pricing
- **Facebook Marketplace** — Local market pricing and demand signals
- **AutoTrader** — Dealer listings and inventory trends
- **iSeeCars** — Historical sales data and depreciation trends
- **Internal Records** — South Central Texas Automotive Group acquisition and resale history

**Target dataset:** 300+ vehicle listings across 8 high-demand makes and models in the 
San Antonio and surrounding Texas market (2024-2026)
